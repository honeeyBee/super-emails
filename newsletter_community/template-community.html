Navigate the Path from Data to Policy with UVA's Schools of Data Science and Leadership and Public Policy
 

"Navigating the Path from Data to Policy" is a one-day course where participants gain fluency in the fundamentals of using data to inform policy research. Topics include understanding causality, combating biases in data collection and analysis, accounting for different interpretations of data, and—most importantly—how data can become policy. The course is offered Oct. 29 in Rosslyn, VA, or online and offered in partnership between UVA's Batten School of Leadership and Public Policy and School of Data Science.
